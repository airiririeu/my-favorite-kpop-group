(G)I-DLE

(G)I-DLE (아이들 / [여자]아이들) currently consists of 5 members: Soyeon, Miyeon, Minnie, Yuqi and Shuhua. They officially debuted on May 2nd, 2018 under Cube Entertainment. Soojin left the group on August 14th, 2021.
